Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect your name, mobile number, delivery address, order details, delivery preference, and messages needed to process KSiraa dairy orders.

How We Use Information

We use your information to create orders, contact you about delivery, send product updates, provide customer support, and maintain order records.

WhatsApp And SMS

When you request OTP login, order updates, or product updates, we may contact you through SMS, phone, app notifications, or WhatsApp using the number you provide.

Payments

If online payments are enabled, payment processing may be handled by a payment provider. KSiraa should not store full card, UPI PIN, or banking credentials in this app.

Data Sharing

We only share data with service providers required for delivery, OTP, WhatsApp messaging, payment processing, hosting, backups, and legal compliance.

Data Retention

We retain order and customer records as needed for business operations, tax/accounting, support, and legal requirements.

Your Choices

You can ask KSiraa to update your details, stop promotional messages, or delete customer details where legally allowed.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact KSiraa through the business phone number shared with customers.
