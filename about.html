About Us

Premium dairy, prepared traditionally and delivered fresh to your door.

Our story

KSiraa was started with a simple idea — that good dairy should taste like it used to. Ghee that smells of slow heat. Butter that's churned, not stirred. Paneer that's soft because it was made this morning. We're a small team working with trusted local dairies to make that simple idea real, one delivery at a time.

What we make

Pure desi ghee, farm-fresh butter, paneer, cottage cheese, and milk — sourced from a handful of dairies we visit ourselves and prepared the same day they go out.

Why we exist

Most dairy you find in stores has been sitting in trucks and shelves for days. We deliver direct, in small daily batches, so the dairy that reaches you is the dairy we made this morning. No preservatives, no shortcuts, no compromises.
